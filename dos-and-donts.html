✅ Do's

  • Use masked Aadhaar (hide first 8 digits) wherever possible
  • Share documents only on official government portals
  • Check for HTTPS and padlock 🔒 in website URLs
  • Use watermarks when sharing via email (e.g., “For KYC only”)

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't post NID photos on social media
  • Don't send ID copies on WhatsApp groups or public forums
  • Never store unprotected copies on shared drives
